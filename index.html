Ride My Way

Get There Fast And Cheap

How it works

As a user, you can add your next road trip on our platform for other interesting users to reach you, you can add the road trip details like a starting point, destination, the date of the road trip, the time, the seat available and the fees for each seat. After that, an interesting user will send you a request to ride with you, you get to accept or reject your road trip ride requests, On the road trip day, the interested users which you accept their requests will meet you at the starting point with their fees. That way you get to go on a road and still earn some cash apart from your wages.

A a user, If you want to take a road trip, and you need a way to get there, all you have to do is search for your destination on our platform, for available ride offers from other users, if you see the one that you like you will send a request to the ride owner to tell him/her that you are interested in the ride offer, if he/she accepts your ride offer you meet the owner on the starting point at the date of the road trip with your fees

Description

Ride My Way is a carpooling service available for all, you get to earn extra cash on every road trip you make.
